Zeppelin How-to:
1. Go to the Zeppelin web-interface: http://130.226.142.166:9995
2. Log in with your itu-email and the password that has been sent to you
3. Download the two intro-notebooks to your local machine: https://github.itu.dk/niec/lsda2020/tree/master/zeppelin-notebooks
4. In the Zeppelin interface press "Import note"
5. Press "Select JSON File" (You do not need to specify a name)
6. Select one of the two notebooks you just downloaded
7. Repeat step 4-6
8. Go through the intro notebook
9. Start working!
